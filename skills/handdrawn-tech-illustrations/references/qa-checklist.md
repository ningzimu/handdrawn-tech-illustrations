# QA Checklist

Run before delivery.

## Content

- One image explains one core idea.
- The image makes the point understandable through a scene, diagram, evidence layer, comparison, relationship, or visual hook.
- The visual approach comes from the source material, not a generic stock composition.
- Claims, metrics, product details, and dates are sourced or marked as assumptions.
- Screenshots or source images are represented accurately.

## Text

- Chinese text is readable, relevant, and not garbled.
- No fake English, fake URLs, gibberish, or meaningless filler labels.
- Text is sparse enough for phone viewing.
- Cover text is readable as a thumbnail.
- WeChat 1:1 uses shorter text and is composed separately.

## Style

- Refined mode: near-white warm paper, fine ink/pencil linework, delicate hatching, compact technical diagram, restrained pastel accents.
- The image does not look like a generic template, unrelated card layout, SaaS dashboard, or decorative poster.
- Multi-image sets share line weight, tone, density, and text scale.

## Composition

- Focal point is clear.
- Body illustration has generous whitespace.
- 3:4 cover has no large under-filled vertical bands.
- No random blobs, stickers, nested cards, heavy shadows, or gradients.
- Supplied screenshots, product UI, code, charts, and hardware details are not accidentally cropped.

## Dimensions

Report actual dimensions honestly.

Expected targets when strict sizing is requested:

- 21:9 article cover: `2520 x 1080`.
- WeChat 21:9: `2100 x 900`.
- WeChat 1:1: `1080 x 1080`.
- Xiaohongshu/Rednote: `1080 x 1440`.
- Body illustration: `1920 x 1080`.

## Final Report

Include:

- Image path or paths.
- Image count and filenames.
- Visual mode used.
- Dimensions checked.
- Verification performed.
- Remaining risks.
